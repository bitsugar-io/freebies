# Privacy Policy for Freebie

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Overview

Freebie ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that helps users discover and track
promotional offers from sports teams. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect
your information.

## Information We Collect

### Device Information

- **Device Identifier**: A randomly generated unique ID to identify your device. This is not linked
  to your Apple ID or any personal information.
- **Platform**: Whether you're using iOS or Android.

### Push Notification Token

- If you enable push notifications, we store your device's push token to send you alerts about
  triggered deals.

### Usage Data

- Which promotional offers you subscribe to
- Which deals you've acknowledged or dismissed

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Name, email, or contact information
- Location data
- Browsing history
- Financial information
- Social media accounts
- Photos or media

## How We Use Your Information

- To deliver push notifications about deals you've subscribed to
- To remember which offers you're tracking
- To improve the app experience

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely on our servers
- Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain
- We use HTTPS for all network communications
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as you use the app
- You can request deletion of your data by contacting us

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

- **Expo Push Notifications**: For delivering push notifications
- **Fly.io**: For hosting our backend servers

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from
children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting
the new policy in the app.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:

**GitHub**: https://github.com/retr0h/freebies/issues

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings
